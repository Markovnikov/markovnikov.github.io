DCS Classes

Prior to starting training, all hopefull foster parents must fill out an inquiry form with DCS. That form is found here.

All foster parents have to attend training classes through DCS to obtain their Foster Parent License. A list of potential classes is below:

  • Trauma Informed Care II

  • Trauma Informed Care I

  • Developmental Disabilities

  • RAPT IV - (must be taken before adoptions can continue!)

  • Adoption Legal Overview


A full training schedule can be found here. or you can contact your foster care specialist for more information!



Drug Screening, Finger Printing and Background Check

DCS in the state of Indiana Requires all potential foster parents to submit to a drug screen, finger printing and a backgrounck check.

You must pay for this yourself!
Below are places that will do your finger prints, drug screen and background check.
Blackbird Clinical Services - Provides drug screens and finger prints.
Indiana Identigo - Inkless - Provides finger printing and background checks.



Preparing for your Homestudy

DCS in Indiana requires all potential foster parents to go though a home study. What this is, is a DCS foster specialist will evaluate the safety and cleanliness of your home!

Below are some tips on how to prepare for the home study!

  1. Ensure your home is neat and clean

  2. Ensure that each child has his or her OWN room - they cannot share.

  3. Ensure each child has their OWN crib or bed.

  4. Ensure your home has been child proofed with plug protectors and baby gates if needed.



Once you have completed all of the above steps, please download and keep for your records the foster parent annual review checklist.

The file is available for download here.
